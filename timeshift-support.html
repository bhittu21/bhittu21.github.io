Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install TimeShift?

TimeShift is available on the Chrome Web Store. Simply click "Add to Chrome" and follow the prompts to install. The extension will appear in your browser toolbar.

Does TimeShift work offline?

Yes! TimeShift works completely offline. All timezone data is stored locally in the extension, so you can use it even without an internet connection.

Does TimeShift collect any data?

No. TimeShift is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personal data, browsing history, or usage statistics. All conversions happen locally on your device.

How do I use TimeShift?

Simply highlight any time or date text on a webpage, right-click, and select "Convert with TimeShift" from the context menu. You can also click the TimeShift icon in your browser toolbar to open the main interface.

Is TimeShift free to use?

Yes, TimeShift is completely free to use. There are no premium features, no subscriptions, and no ads. It's a free, open-source project.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues with TimeShift, please email us at sheikhabirali@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your browser version and operating system.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests. Please email us at sheikhabirali@gmail.com with your feature ideas, and we'll consider them for future updates.
